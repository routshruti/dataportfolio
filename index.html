About Me

Analytical and Applied Economics Postgraduate | Data Storyteller

As an Economics student, I have always been fascinated by the impact of data on economic models. During my final year of study, I worked extensively with datasets for my dissertation. This involved months of collecting, cleaning, and analyzing data, followed by writing a comprehensive report and presenting my findings. This hands-on experience sparked my interest in data analysis, so I explored the field further and realized that it aligned perfectly with my analytical background. Then what? I knew this was the path for me. My journey since then has been one of constant learning and growth.

Feel free to explore my portfolio to see some of the projects that reflect my abilities and dedication to the field. I am excited about the opportunity to apply these skills in a professional setting and contribute to data-driven decision-making.

This comprehensive approach enables a thorough evaluation of business performance and key strengths, supporting effective strategic planning and growth initiatives.
